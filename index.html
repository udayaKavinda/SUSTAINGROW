About Us

Independent quality inspection for global buyers.

SUSTAINGROW (PVT) LTD is a Sri Lanka-based independent quality inspection company specializing in coco peat and coir-based growing media. We support international buyers by ensuring products meet required quality standards before shipment.

With deep industry knowledge and hands-on production experience, our team performs on-site factory inspections, process verification, and technical quality checks to reduce risk before export.
